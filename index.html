Food and beverges

We serve quality and healthy food where we serve a buffet during lunchtime,breakfast and dinner. There is an open bar every night from 8:00pm. Our dishes are also vegeterian friendly.

The Hotel has a rooftop heated swimming pool, has a safe playground for children to play, a game drive everyday around the nearby game reserve which will include a tourguide. For the party-lovers we have very good and entertaining djs in the bar.

Room service

Room Service is very efficient in delivering food up to the room served hot or cold depending on your preference. We offer laundry and ironing services of your clothes. We also clean the room everyday.

Each room has various featues such as free wifi, An AC for hot and cold settings. Bathtubs and Netflix payed subscription. Some rooms have a good sunset view.
